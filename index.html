      Schedule
      —

      One week at a time — each listing has its own code, QR, and log, and maps 1-to-1 to an NI entry. Edit changes title, time, site, credit, or Q-eligibility in place.

      Clean up duplicate listings

      Finds listings that repeat the same date, type, title, and site. Removing them only ever deletes empty copies — any copy with recorded check-ins is kept, and at least one copy of every offering survives. Resident sign-ins are never touched.

      Add a conference
      Conference added. Its rotating code and QR are live on the Presenter tab.
      Generate the year · weekly template

      Stamps the standing week across a date range: Mon Intern Report · Tue Noon Conference (residents pick Goldman / Sim / South Campus Special at check-in) · Wed Ambulatory Half Day (single check-in; Grand Rounds rides along as cancelled + Q-credit through the hiatus) · Thu AHD Hours 1–3 · Fri Noon Conference (Resident Report / Sim choice). Edit WEEKLY_TEMPLATE / HIATUS in Code.gs when the week changes. Safe to re-run; existing listings are skipped.

      Review queue

      Q-credit submissions and any flagged check-ins land here — each shows its uploaded screenshot(s) as a thumbnail (tap to open full size). Approve keeps credit; Deny removes it.

      No entries awaiting review.

      PEAC · clinic week review

      This week's clinic group, their assigned modules, and who has submitted. Thumbnails link to the full screenshot; Deny removes an optional module's conference credit.

      AHD attendance check

      A half-day only counts when all three hours are checked in. Residents with 1–2 of 3 hours on an AHD date are usually a missed scan, not a real absence — review them here and credit the missing hour(s) if appropriate.

      Credit totals · House Cup tally

      One point per conference attended (each AHD hour is its own point). The AHD columns track the program requirement separately: a half-day counts only when all three hours are attended, with a goal of ≥50% of offered half-days.

      Morning report · automated

      Runs server-side at 06:00, covering the previous day — Q-credit submissions received through the grace period are included. Weekends are skipped; Monday's report covers Friday through Sunday. Nothing is sent on days with no recorded credits. Recipient and send hour are set in Code.gs → CONFIG.
